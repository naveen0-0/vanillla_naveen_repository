About Me

Features & Functionality
  1. Authentication Setup (SignUp and Login).
  2. First Signup and create an account.
  3. You will be redirected to the login page.
  4. Login with your credentials.
  5. Go to the "THIRD" tab (Add Friend tab) and search for you friend.
  6. Hit the send button to send a friend request (Create another user in a private window to simulate this experience).
  7. Your friend will receive your friend request (which can be "ACCEPTED" or "DECLINED").
  8. If accepted, Your friend will be added in your friends list in the first tab.
  9. You can send messages back and forth with your friend.
  10. In the second tab, you can enter a group name and create a group.
  11. And in the top right, you can click the icon and your friend to the group and have group conversations.
  12. By the way, LOGOUT button is in the bottom left corner.
